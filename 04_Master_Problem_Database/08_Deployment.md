# Deployment Problems

**Category ID:** C-08

**Status:** Active

---

# Purpose

This document contains validated problems related to deploying, hosting, monitoring, scaling, and operating AI-assisted software projects.

Many applications work correctly in development but fail after deployment due to missing configurations, infrastructure mistakes, poor monitoring, or operational oversights. This document focuses on helping developers confidently move projects from development to production.
