# Purpose

This document contains validated problems related to product strategy, business decisions, user validation, pricing, market positioning, and long-term product success.

Building technically impressive software is only one part of creating a successful product. Many projects fail because they solve the wrong problem, target the wrong audience, or lack a sustainable business strategy.

This document focuses on helping developers think like product builders and founders, not just engineers.
