# Purpose

This document contains validated software engineering problems that do not fit neatly into a single category or span multiple categories.

Some challenges affect planning, architecture, security, tooling, and product strategy simultaneously. These cross-cutting problems are documented here until they can be better categorized or permanently remain as general engineering concerns.

This document also serves as an incubator for newly discovered problems that require further research.
